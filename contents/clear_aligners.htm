The Clear Aligners option in the function panel allows you to accurately plan treatment for further production of aligners. To plan the treatment, go through the workflow described below.

 

Note: The Clear Aligners option requires a specific license activation. Contact your 3Shape reseller to activate additional modules.

 

Note: You can also open Clear Aligners from the patient browser by selecting the required model set and clicking the Clear Aligners icon.

 

 

The Clear Aligners workflow includes preparation steps, where the occlusion is aligned, planes are set, and segmentation is done.

 

After this, you can perform arch analysis and move teeth for aligners treatment.

 

Define an ideal dental arch for each jaw (see the chapter Analysis).

 

Use virtual setups to create a sequence of teeth movements for further aligners' production based on the exported models (see the chapter Treatment).

 

Once done, click Next to continue to review step.

 

Generate the information you need (Preparation and Analysis sections) into a printable document.

 

There is the possibility to either send the Communicate™ cases to Treatment Review platform by clicking the Send for review button or to generate data on Clear Aligners Treatment Plan by clicking the Export for review button.

 

Note: Send for Review and Export for Review options are the add-on to Clear Aligner Studio and require a specific license activation. Contact your 3Shape reseller to activate additional modules.
