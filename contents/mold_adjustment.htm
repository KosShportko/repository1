3.8.1 Clear Aligners Mold Adjustment

Note: To use Mold Adjustment, ensure that the appropriate licenses are enabled on your dongle. Contact your 3Shape reseller to learn more.

 

When the Virtual Setup Export is completed, the Mold Adjustment application starts automatically.

 

 

The Mold Adjustment application provides you with the following tools:

 

Wax - allows you to fill in the hollow areas on a model with material for better placement and shape. You can apply it separately per a maxillary and mandibular jaw and control the amount of filling with the corresponding sliders. To change the transparency of areas filled with material, use the visibility sliders on the right side of the screen.

 

Under the Wax tab, the following menu items are available:

 

  • Reinforcement - is used to fill in the flat spots where teeth are missing.
  •   Wax-up:
    • Overhang wax-up - is used to fill in the areas where teeth overhang over the gingiva. To apply wax to the concerned area, select the required tooth/teeth.
    • Interproximal wax-up - is used to fill in the areas between neighboring teeth.
    •  Gingiva line wax-up - is used to fill in the areas where gingiva extends on teeth.

Cut line - allows you to manually edit a cut-line per each jaw. Click and draw the part of the cut-line that requires correction and the line will be adjusted.

 

 

Hollowing - allows you to hollow out the inside of the model to save material consumption while retaining the needed structural stability. Before hollowing, you can choose a machine and material, and the software will suggest the recommended wall thickness values.

 

  • Drain holes – allows you to insert the drain holes on the models for better print quality and easier post processing. You can adjust the radius of the drain holes, as well as their position and number.

 

Global adjustments

 

In the Global adjustments mode, you can review all the exported models, and switch among molds at the bottom of the screen. The tools and their settings are applied to all molds.

 

 

  Individual adjustments

 

In the Individual adjustments mode, the tools and their settings are applied per each mold individually.

 

 

Save

 

When all the changes are done, click the Save button to automatically save the models into the location selected for export in Virtual Setup in .dxf and .pts formats.
