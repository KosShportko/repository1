The Ortho System is a software medical device used for:

  • Importing 3D scanned orthodontic models of full arch mandibular and maxillary dental arches with their topographical characteristics
  • Accessing 3D scanned orthodontic models of the patients
  • Measure, analyze, inspect and visualize 3D scanned orthodontic models
  • Design orthodontic appliances based on 3D scanned orthodontic models
