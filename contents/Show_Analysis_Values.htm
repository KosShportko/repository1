The 3D measurements and custom analysis option allows you to perform and edit the desired 3D measurements on the model.

 

 

Combination

Description

Ctrl + click of the mouse on the landmark

Allows you to make a measurement based on the selected landmark (angle or distance).

Alt + click of the mouse

Allows you to place a landmark close to the existing one (within the highlighter radius).

 

measurement tools

Angles
Planes
Measures (direct)
Measures (caliper)
Distance to line
Distance to plane

Custom splines

 

Click the Collections icon to open the appropriate tab. Follow the instructions displayed at the top of the window.

 

Click the Start button to begin the process. The results of the measurements performed on the model will be displayed in the table.

 

You can access and edit your collections on the Settings page by going to Settings > Custom Analysis > Collections.
