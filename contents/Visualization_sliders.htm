Slider Description

Maxilla - allows you to show/hide as well as adjust the transparency level of the maxillary model.

Mandible - allows you to show/hide as well as adjust the transparency level of the mandibulary model.

Show all teeth axes - displays all teeth axes or hides them.

Show tooth axis angle - allows you to display the axis angles of the selected tooth.

CT - allows you to adjust the visualization of CT scan.

Skin alpha value - allows you to customize the way the skin is displayed.

Bones alpha value - allows you to adjust the visualization of the bones of the scan.

Enamel alpha value - controls the way the enamel is visualized.

Teeth - allows you to adjust the transparency level of the teeth, or even turn their visualization off.

Tissue - turns on/of the visualization of the tissue and allows to adjust its transparency.

Selected tooth - allows to change the transparency of the selected tooth.

Show original position of teeth - when turned on, highlights the original position (malocclusion) of the moved teeth in the transparent blue color.

Show mirror for selected tooth - when turned on, shows a mirrored image of the selected tooth along the sagittal plane.

Show tooth markers - shows/hides the markers on the teeth.

Mark modified teeth - when turned on, indicates with green marks those teeth which have already been moved in a setup or subsetup.

Show all teeth landmarks - shows/hides the landmarks on all teeth.

Show symmetry lines for standard views - allows you to show/hide as well as adjust the symmetry lines across the model.
