The Appliance Designer software is only intended to be used with the approved range of production equipment and materials provided in the workflows for the relevant approved appliance types.

 

The appliances should only be produced on the machines listed in the software and in this manual, and for the case of the additive 3D printers, the provided CAM settings must be used in manufacturing.

 

Aligners must be produced by vacuum pressing 3rd party NXC approved materials over the dental cast produced from the Clear Aligner, vacuum pressed workflow. The following table states the material cleared for this indication:
